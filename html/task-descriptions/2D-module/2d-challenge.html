Rosa has been given additional tasks of picking up samples from two small islands on the river as she goes across. The figure to the right shows the location of the islands in the river.


Can you build a simulation that shows the trajectory that Rosa’s boat should take in going from her starting position to island 1, then island 1 to island 2, and finally to the airstrip on the other side of the river?


  • Step One: Plan Your Model

    In the conceptual model, select the objects needed to complete the task as well as each objects’ properties and/or behaviors. Keep in mind what was needed to complete previous river tasks to support your conceptual planning.
  • Step Two: Build Your Model

    Program Description

    • Program the boat to start at the loading dock, travel to island 1, then to island 2, and finally - the unloading dock on the other side of the river.
    • Be sure to keep track of the data available in the graph.
    • Would your program work given different locations of the islands?
